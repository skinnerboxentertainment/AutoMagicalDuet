# Worldbuilding Consistency

Narrative content needs explicit canon status, world rules, contradictions checks, and player relevance.

## Patterns

- Assign each lore entry a canon level: Established, Provisional, or Under Review.
- Document what is possible and impossible in the world.
- Cross-reference new lore against existing lore before accepting it.
- Give mysteries true answers even when players never learn them.
- Define faction motivations, relationships, and power structures logically.
- Include player relevance for world elements.

## Anti-patterns

- Lore that contradicts established world rules without review.
- Mysteries with no internal answer.
- Factions that exist only as labels, without motivation or power structure.
- Narrative scope added without production approval.
