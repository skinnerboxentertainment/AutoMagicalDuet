# Core Loops

Every mechanic must connect to at least one loop: a moment-to-moment micro-loop, a 5-15 minute meso-loop, or a session-level macro-loop.

## Patterns

- Define a 30-second micro-loop around intrinsically satisfying player action and immediate feedback.
- Define a meso-loop around goal pursuit, reward, escalation, and renewed intent.
- Define a macro-loop around progression, a natural stopping point, and a reason to return.
- Tie rewards to the loop they reinforce; not every reward belongs in long-term progression.
- Identify what player decision starts, sustains, and ends each loop.

## Anti-patterns

- Mechanics that exist outside all loops.
- Rewards that interrupt the loop they are supposed to reinforce.
- Session structures with no stopping point.
- Progression systems that only increase numbers without changing goals, choices, or mastery.
